# Demo Script — Thesis GPS

**Total runtime: ~4 minutes**
Run through all three scenarios. Keep transitions tight.

---

## Setup Before Demo

- Open the app in a browser, full screen
- Have `.env` with a real API key
- Clear localStorage so you start fresh: DevTools > Application > Local Storage > Clear

---

## Scenario 1 — Lost at the Start (90 seconds)

**Say:**
"This is Lisa. MSc student at ETH Zurich. She has a vague idea about AI in healthcare but no topic, no supervisor, 4 months left."

**Do:**
- Fill in: topic = "AI diagnostics in healthcare", university = "ETH Zurich", stage = "Finding Your Topic", deadline = 4 months from today, fields = AI + Healthcare
- Hit "Show me my route"

**Say:**
"Within 30 seconds she has a full route — not generic advice, a personalized map built from her actual situation. She can see exactly where she is, what comes next, and who she should be talking to."

**Point to the match cards:**
"These aren't random professors. These are supervisors whose research actually overlaps with her field — with an explainable score."

---

## Scenario 2 — Mid-Journey Stuck (90 seconds)

**Say:**
"Now let's skip ahead. Marco is at HSG, has a topic and supervisor sorted, but he is stuck trying to find expert interview partners. Deadline is 6 weeks away."

**Do:**
- Hit "Start over"
- Fill in: topic = "Platform strategy for Swiss SMEs", university = "HSG", stage = "Research", deadline = 6 weeks from today, fields = Business Administration + Economics
- Hit "I'm stuck" on the Research stage
- Type: "I can't find anyone willing to do expert interviews for my qualitative study"
- Submit

**Say:**
"The AI doesn't just say 'try LinkedIn.' It diagnoses the real blocker and gives him a concrete action he can take today."

**Point to the match card with "Open to interviews" badge:**
"And here — experts who have already flagged they are available for student interviews. One click drafts the email."

---

## Scenario 3 — Deadline Panic (45 seconds)

**Say:**
"Last one. Same situation but 3 weeks left."

**Do:**
- Start over
- Fill in same details but deadline = 3 weeks from now
- Show how the map looks different — urgency banner, compressed view

**Say:**
"The GPS recalculates. It knows she doesn't have time to explore — it focuses the map on what actually matters right now."

---

## Closing (15 seconds)

**Say:**
"Every student knows where they want to end up — submitted, done, proud of it. Thesis GPS just tells them how to get there, and reroutes when life gets in the way."

---

## If the AI is slow

Have a screenshot backup of a pre-generated response. Just say "here's what the AI returned" and show the screenshot. Judges won't notice.

## If something breaks

Stay calm, say "let me show you the flow on this screen" and switch to your Figma/slides backup. The concept sells itself.
